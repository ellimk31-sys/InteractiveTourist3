A Digital Welcome to Viitasaari

This is an interactive proposal for a cost-effective, innovative, and sustainable digital solution to enhance the tourist experience and support local businesses in Viitasaari, without the need for dedicated staff.

Prepared by: Elli

elli.mk31@gmail.com

The Viitasaari Digital Tourist Ecosystem

Our solution is a multi-faceted digital ecosystem built on three interconnected pillars. It's designed to be comprehensive yet simple, providing tourists with everything they need to explore Viitasaari while actively promoting local businesses.

Pillar 1: Interactive QR Code Trail & Digital Guidebook

This foundational element transforms physical locations into gateways to rich digital content. QR codes placed at key points of interest direct tourists to a mobile-friendly "Digital Guidebook," offering context-aware information, local stories, and a clear link to our Virtual Assistant for more questions.

  • Provides relevant information based on the tourist's current location.
  • Actively recommends nearby cafes, shops, and services.
  • User-friendly: requires no app download, just a smartphone camera.

Pillar 2: Community-Powered Interactive Map

A central, visual hub for exploring all of Viitasaari's offerings. This dynamic, categorized map visually represents points of interest, businesses, and services, making discovery intuitive and engaging. It directly addresses the proposal's call for "interactive maps."

  • Allows tourists to visually discover what's available and plan their routes.
  • Empowers local businesses by giving them direct visibility.
  • Simple to maintain with community input, ensuring long-term accuracy.

Pillar 3: AI-Powered Virtual Assistant on Telegram

For instant, 24/7 conversational support and personalized recommendations. Tourists can ask questions in a familiar chat interface and receive rich media responses, including map links and photos, creating a powerful, staff-less support system.

  • Provides constant, interactive assistance without dedicated personnel.
  • Uses a platform many international tourists already have installed.
  • Persistent chat history allows users to refer back to information.
